ðŸ”¹ General HR Questions

  • Tell me about yourself.
  • What are your strengths and weaknesses?
  • Why should we hire you?
  • Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
  • Why do you want to work with our company?
  • What motivates you?
  • Describe a challenge you faced and how you solved it.
  • How do you handle stress and pressure?
  • Are you willing to relocate or travel for work?
  • Do you prefer working in a team or independently?

ðŸ”¹ Freshers / Students

  • Tell me about your academic background.
  • What projects have you worked on during college?
  • What internships or training programs have you completed?
  • How do you keep your technical knowledge updated?
  • What are your career goals?
  • Which subjects are you strongest in?
  • What extracurricular activities were you involved in?
  • How do you handle group projects?

ðŸ”¹ Technical (IT Roles)

  • What programming languages are you comfortable with?
  • Explain OOPS concepts.
  • What is the difference between Java and Python?
  • What is a database? Explain normalization.
  • What is an API?
  • Explain the software development life cycle (SDLC).
  • What are the differences between frontend and backend development?
  • What is cloud computing?
  • Explain Agile methodology.
  • What projects from your resume are you most proud of?

ðŸ”¹ For Experienced Candidates

  • Tell me about your previous job role.
  • What was your biggest achievement in your last position?
  • How do you manage deadlines?
  • Have you ever managed a team? How was the experience?
  • Why are you leaving your current company?
  • How do you resolve conflicts in a team?
  • What leadership qualities do you have?
  • How do you adapt to changes in the workplace?
