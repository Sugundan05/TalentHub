Aptitude Methods & Formulas

🔢 Numbers & Arithmetic

  • HCF × LCM = Product of numbers
  • Divisibility Rules (2,3,5,9,11...)
  • Remainder Theorem: Dividend = Divisor × Quotient + Remainder

📊 Percentages

  • Percentage = (Value ÷ Total) × 100
  • Successive % Change = (x + y + xy/100)%

⚖️ Ratio & Proportion

  • a/b = c/d → ad = bc
  • Direct proportion: both increase/decrease together
  • Inverse proportion: one increases, other decreases

📌 Averages

  • Average = (Sum ÷ Number)
  • Weighted Avg = (Σwx ÷ Σw)

💰 Profit & Loss

  • Profit = SP – CP
  • Profit % = (Profit ÷ CP) × 100
  • Loss % = (Loss ÷ CP) × 100

🏦 Interest

  • SI = (P × R × T)/100
  • CI = P(1 + R/100)^T – P

⏱️ Time, Speed & Distance

  • Speed = Distance ÷ Time
  • Relative Speed = (Sum or Difference of speeds)

⚙️ Time & Work

  • If A does work in x days → Efficiency = 1/x
  • Total Time = 1 / (1/x + 1/y)

🔀 Permutations & Combinations

  • nPr = n! ÷ (n–r)!
  • nCr = n! ÷ (r!(n–r)!)

🎲 Probability

  • P(E) = Favorable outcomes ÷ Total outcomes

📐 Mensuration

  • Circle: A = πr², Circumference = 2πr
  • Triangle: A = ½ × b × h
  • Sphere: V = 4/3 πr³

🧮 Algebra

  • Quadratic: x = (–b ± √(b² – 4ac)) ÷ 2a
  • (a+b)² = a² + 2ab + b²
  • (a–b)² = a² – 2ab + b²
